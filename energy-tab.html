why Solar

Homeowners use solar and Powerwall to generate, store and manage their own power supply. Tesla Solar Panels capture free, clean energy from the sun that you can use to power your home. Powerwall works as a home battery to store excess energy for use at night, during an outage or when grid prices are high. Together, solar and Powerwall can power your home 24/7 and save you money on electricity.

Energy Incentives

Depending on your location, state and local utility incentives may be available for Powerwall and Tesla Solar Panels. Most rebates can either be claimed after purchase or reflected as a reduction in the price of your purchase.
